## Why web analytics is crucial in 2026

### Data at the core of marketing decisions

In 2026, **web analytics** is the foundation of every digital marketing strategy:

- **ROI measurement**: every euro invested in SEO, SEA, or content must be measurable and correlated with business results
- **Real-time steering**: analytics dashboards enable continuous strategy adjustments
- **Multi-touch attribution**: understanding the complete visitor journey, from first interaction to conversion
- **GDPR compliance**: data collection must comply with European regulations

### Analytics and GEO: measuring AI visibility

**[GEO](/en/blog/what-is-geo/)** creates a new measurement need: traffic referred by generative AI. In 2026, visits from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews represent a growing share of organic traffic. Analytics tools must be configured to **track these new sources** and measure their business contribution.

To understand how AI engines select their sources, see our article on [techniques to appear on ChatGPT](/en/blog/techniques-to-appear-on-chatgpt/).

### The post-Universal Analytics era

The migration to **GA4** is complete, but many businesses still underutilize the platform's capabilities: event-based data models, predictive audiences, BigQuery integration, advanced explorations. Analytics agencies help fully exploit GA4's potential.

## Criteria for selecting a web analytics agency

### Essential skills

- **GA4 mastery**: advanced configuration, custom events, predictive audiences, BigQuery integration
- **Tag Management**: GTM Server-Side, consent mode, structured data layer
- **Data visualization**: exploitable and actionable Looker Studio, Tableau, or Power BI dashboards
- **GDPR / Privacy**: consent management (CMP), anonymization, regulatory compliance
- **Data engineering**: BigQuery architectures, Cloud Functions, ETL pipelines
- **Attribution**: multi-touch models, incrementality measurement

### Quality signals to verify

- **Certifications**: Google Marketing Platform Partner, Adobe Analytics Certified
- **Documented case studies** with measurable business impact
- **Methodology**: tagging plan process, documentation, testing
- **Training**: ability to train internal teams for autonomy
- **Expertise in [structured data](/en/blog/structured-data-schema-org-guide/)** to enrich collection and analysis
