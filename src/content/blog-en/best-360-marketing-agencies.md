## Why a 360 marketing approach is essential in 2026

### User journey fragmentation

In 2026, the average purchase journey involves **7 to 12 touchpoints** before conversion: Google search, AI Overviews, social media, email, display retargeting, organic content, customer reviews. A 360 approach ensures **message consistency** and **lever complementarity** at every funnel stage.

### The GEO era changes the game

The emergence of **[GEO](/en/blog/what-is-geo/)** (Generative Engine Optimization) adds a new channel to 360 marketing. Generative AIs (Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity) are becoming full-fledged traffic sources, with referred traffic up **527%** in 2026. 360 agencies that integrate GEO into their arsenal cover the entire digital visibility spectrum.

To learn more, see our article on [how to optimize content for generative AI](/en/blog/optimize-content-for-generative-ai/).

### Cross-channel synergies

The value of a 360 approach lies in **cross-channel synergies**:

- **SEO + SEA**: identifying keywords where SEO can replace SEA to save budget
- **Content + SEO + GEO**: creating expert content that ranks organically and is cited by AI
- **Analytics + all levers**: unified data driving real-time budget allocation
- **Social + content**: amplifying SEO content reach via social media
- **[Structured data](/en/blog/structured-data-schema-org-guide/) + SEO + GEO**: Schema.org markup boosting SERP and AI response visibility

## Criteria for selecting a 360 marketing agency

### Essential skills

- **Lever coverage**: SEO, SEA, content, social, analytics — and ideally GEO
- **Unified strategy**: a holistic vision rather than skill silos
- **Data and measurement**: advanced analytics, multi-touch attribution, consolidated reporting
- **Technical expertise**: structured data, tag management, web performance
- **Adaptability**: ability to reallocate budgets between levers based on performance
- **Industry knowledge**: experience in your industry

### Quality signals to verify

- **Certifications**: Google Premier Partner, Meta Business Partner, analytics certifications
- **Documented case studies** with cross-channel metrics (not just one lever)
- **Methodology**: strategic planning and cross-lever steering approach
- **Dedicated team**: multi-skilled consultants rather than isolated specialists
- **[E-E-A-T](/en/blog/e-e-a-t-and-geo/)** and GEO expertise to maximize AI visibility
